---
title: "Farcaster Integration"
description: "Login with Farcaster and connect your Pinata account"
---

Pinata allows you to connect your Farcaster account to your Pinata account which can be used for [Frame Analytics](/farcaster/fdk#frame-analytics). To get started visit the [Integrations page](https://app.pinata.cloud/integrations) by clicking on the profile button in the top right, then selecting Integrations.

<img style={{ width: '100%', borderRadius: '0.5rem'}} src="https://docs.mypinata.cloud/ipfs/bafkreidagjp2i7gcnigg6z7n24w4rbpwbexk35fsftbzr6q3owmkvvu7pi
" />

Once there you can click on "Sign In" which will bring up a QR code you can scan with you phone. 

<Note>You will need [Warpcast](https://warpcast.com) installed on your phone to sign in</Note>

Scanning the QR code with your phone should prompt you to open Warpcast where you can sign in and approve the integration. 

After signing in you can now link your Pinata account with [Frame Analytics](/farcaster/fdk#frame-analytics)!
